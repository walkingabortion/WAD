About

  Fresh Socks is a project created not only for people interested in reading more, but also for the people who are in need of space in their bookshelf for a new book and are happy to trade away their old books.

  Having the customer satisfaction in mind, Fresh Socks is created in order for the customer to be able to judge the quality of the book he wants to pay for by having the opportunity to look at pictures of the product, get a detailed report about the state of the product and also being able to judge a seller's credibility by their rating.
